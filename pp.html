Auto-Ping Bot Privacy Policy

  1. Information Collected

    Auto-Ping Bot does not collect personal information beyond what Discord provides when interacting with the Bot (e.g., usernames, IDs, guild IDs, channel IDs).

  2. Usage of Information

    Information is used solely to perform Bot functions, such as sending auto-pings, generating status messages, and reporting issues to the support server.

  3. Data Storage

    - Channel configurations and ping delays are stored in a local JSON file.
    - Support reports submitted via the Bot are sent to a designated channel in the support server.
    - No information is sold, shared, or used for marketing.

  4. Data Retention

    Data is retained as long as the Bot is active in your server or until the server owner deletes the configuration.

  5. Security

    The Bot implements reasonable measures to protect your data but cannot guarantee complete security.

  6. Third-Party Services

    The Bot may integrate with Discord’s API. Please review Discord’s privacy policies as well.

  7. Changes

    The developer may update this Privacy Policy. Users will be notified through the support server or Top.gg listing when major changes occur.
